# March 2026 Bet Trends Report

**Date:** 2026-04-01
**Window:** 2026-03-01 to 2026-03-31
**Platforms:** Omen (Gnosis Chain) + Polymarket (Polygon)

---

## Executive Summary

Both platforms saw significant growth in March. Omen daily bets nearly **5x'd** from ~600/day to ~3,000+/day. Polymarket grew more modestly but saw agent count nearly **double**. Accuracy on both platforms dipped in the second half of the month.

### Key Patterns

**Volume ramp:** Bets jumped from ~600-1,000/day in early March to 2,000-3,000+/day by mid-month and stayed there. The step-change happened around Mar 5 (from 1,042 to 2,129).

**Agent growth:** Active daily agents grew from 77 (Mar 1) to 220+ (late March) — a ~3x increase.

**Accuracy dip mid-month:** The Mar 16-18 window had a notable accuracy drop (49.6%, 41.6%, 45.8%) before recovering. This aligns with the week-compare analysis showing a -0.8pp accuracy drop in the second half.

**Bet sizes shrinking:** Average bet trended down from 0.06-0.17 xDAI early month to 0.06-0.08 xDAI late month. More agents placing smaller bets.

### Key Patterns

**Steady volume growth:** Daily bets grew from ~250-290/day (early March) to ~300-425/day (mid-to-late March). Not as dramatic as Omen's 5x jump, but consistent.

**Agent fleet nearly doubled:** Active daily agents went from 14 (Mar 1) to 79 (Mar 25) — new PolyStrat agents onboarding steadily through the month.

**Bet size creeping up:** Average bet drifted from ~$2.20 to ~$2.80 over the month. Agents are getting slightly more aggressive.

**Share prices dropping late month:** Average share price fell from 0.65-0.72 to 0.50-0.56 in the last 4 days of March. Agents are buying cheaper shares (further from consensus), which means either contrarian bets or less liquid markets.

**Weekend dips:** Consistent drops on weekends (Mar 8, Mar 22, Mar 29) — fewer bets but similar agent counts. Markets may have fewer active questions on weekends.

## Cross-Platform Comparison

| Metric              | Omen              | Polymarket         |
|---------------------|------------------:|--------------------|
| Total bets (March)  | ~74,900           | ~9,340             |
| Avg bets/day        | ~2,400            | ~300               |
| Active agents (peak)| 226               | 79                 |
| Bet size (median)   | 0.025 xDAI (~$0.025) | $2.50 USDC     |
| Volume growth       | ~5x (600 to 3,000+)| ~1.7x (250 to 425)|
| Agent growth        | ~3x (77 to 220)  | ~5x (14 to 79)    |

Omen has **8x more bets** but at **100x smaller sizes**. The median Omen bet ($0.025) is essentially a dust bet — high frequency, low conviction. Polymarket agents bet $2.50 per position, more like real trading.

Both platforms saw strong agent onboarding. Polymarket had the larger proportional agent growth (5x vs 3x) but from a much smaller base.
